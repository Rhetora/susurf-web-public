What kit do SuSurf Have?

SuSurf has a large amount of kit that can be borrowed free of charge by its members! We have a large collection of boards, wetsuits, and other misc. gear for member use.

  • Large Foamie Boards
  • Hard Minimals (Bics)
  • Foamie Shortboards
  • Fibreglass Shortboards
  • Mens and Womens Winter Wetsuits
  • Leashes
  • Soft Roof Racks

All kit is available to borrow to paid members, however if there is a club trip, this will take priority on kit required.
